The Historic 2026 Squad

A generation-defining team that captured the hearts of the Dominican nation

The 2026 World Baseball Classic represented a convergence of Dominican talent unlike anything the sport had seen before. For the first time, the likes of Juan Soto, Julio Rodríguez, and Vladimir Guerrero Jr. shared the same dugout, united under the Quisqueya flag.

This was not merely a national team — it was a Dream Team. A roster assembled from the very best the Dominican Republic had produced across generations, from veteran legends to the brightest young stars in the sport today.

Although the championship eluded them in a thrilling final against Japan, the 2026 squad wrote a chapter in Dominican baseball history that will be recounted for decades to come.
