Site Name

Name Dominican Power 2026 – The Dream Team Journey

"Dominican Power" fuses the fierce national identity of the Dominican Republic with a declaration of athletic dominance — the same energy that fans feel when the Quisqueya flag fills the stadium. "2026" anchors the site to the historic World Baseball Classic edition, and "The Dream Team Journey" frames the narrative arc of the entire project: this was not just a tournament, it was a generation-defining moment for Dominican baseball.

Optional domain: dominicanpower2026.com

Site Purpose

Dominican Power 2026 is a high-performance digital roster and analytics platform dedicated to the Dominican Republic National Team at the 2026 World Baseball Classic. The site serves as both a fan tribute and a data hub, providing:

  • A dynamic landing page celebrating the historic 2026 squad with a full tournament recap and spotlight on the team's "Big Three" hitters.
  • A fully data-driven roster directory featuring 15+ player cards generated from a local JSON file, each displaying batting average or ERA, MLB team affiliation, player photo, and lineup role.
  • A Game Day Analytics page where users can explore specific matchups via accessible modal dialogs showing box scores, the Player of the Game, and the key turning point of each match.
  • A "My Starting Nine" feature powered by Local Storage, allowing fans to build and persist their own custom dream lineup across browser sessions.

Scenarios

These questions represent the typical visitor to Dominican Power 2026 — passionate fans, baseball analysts, and WBC followers:

Who were the starting pitchers for the Dominican Republic in their semifinal matchup, and what were their tournament stats?
Which players on the 2026 roster had a batting average above .300 during the WBC, and what positions did they play?
How can I build and save my own Dream Starting Nine from the Dominican roster to share with friends?
What happened in the DR vs. USA game — who was the Player of the Game and what was the turning point of the match?

Color Scheme

The palette is drawn directly from the Dominican Republic national flag — deep blue, bold red, and clean white — enriched with a championship gold accent that evokes prestige and the WBC trophy.

Dominican Blue  #002D62 Primary brand color. Used for the header background, section top borders, heading text, nav bar, and footer. Establishes authority and national identity.
Dominican Red  #CE1126 Action and energy color. Used for the hero section, call-to-action buttons, left-border scenario accents, section top borders on key pages, and the footer top stripe.
Championship Gold  #D4AF37 Prestige accent. Used for the site name highlight in the header, nav bottom border, "Big Three" player card borders, badge labels, and key stat highlights.
Ice Blue  #F0F4FA Page background. A cool near-white derived from the blue family. Used as the body background and secondary card surfaces for a clean, fresh feel.
White  #FFFFFF Content surface color. Used as the background for all section cards, player cards, and modal dialogs. Represents the white cross at the center of the Dominican flag.
Near Black  #0A0A0A Body text and footer background. Ensures maximum contrast and readability across all content areas.

Typography

Two Google Fonts are selected to balance athletic boldness with editorial clarity, reflecting the dual nature of the site as both a fan tribute and a data-driven platform.

  • Oswald (Bold 700 / Regular 400) — Used for all headings (h1–h3), the site title, navigation links, player card names, stat labels, and button text. Its condensed, impactful letterforms feel native to sports branding and stadium signage. Applied throughout this site plan document.
  • Source Sans 3 (Regular 400 / SemiBold 600) — Used for all body copy, player card descriptions, scenario text, modal dialog content, and form labels. Its clean humanist design ensures excellent readability at small sizes across mobile and desktop, especially in data-heavy layouts. Applied to all paragraph text in this document.
